## GENERATION SETTINGS RECOMMENDATIONS

**For Higgsfield Generation:**
- **Resolution:** 2K minimum (will scale down)
- **Polygon Count:** Low-medium (optimized for web)
- **Texture Quality:** Medium (web-optimized)
- **Animation FPS:** 30fps

- **Transparency:** Enable alpha channel
- **Compression:** Enable for web delivery
